## Overview

Amazon The Drop was Amazon fashion's attempt to do made on demand street style clothing inspired by geniune influencers.
We worked with Instagram/TikTok influencers to design street style women's clothing and sell it in limited edition "Drops". Making items on demand to reduce waste.

I joined the team as one of a founding engineers, when project was still in PRFAQ stage. I developed key components of the system from front-end to supply chain integration, instagram intregation, and notification system.

From launching The Drop in 2019, to scaling it to half a million subscribers and celebrity launches like with Kylie and Kendall Jenner, it was quite a journey.

I was also responsible to launch internal tooling called Design Central, which was cross-colalboration tool between influencer, manufacturer, and amazon to streamlines design process.

Unfortunately Amazon decided to sunset The Drop in Q1 2024.

## Technologies

React, Java, AuthN (Cognito), GraphQL, ECS, Lambda, DynamoDB, AWS Pinpoint
